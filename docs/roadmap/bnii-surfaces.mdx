# BNII surfaces

Five surfaces, one intelligence asset, increasing depth:

## 1. Credits

Metered like every enterprise AI product. Buy a credit pool; draw it down by what each query actually fires. **The audit log Aria will emit for credibility becomes the billing ledger.** Build them as one system.

## 2. Build on top

Customers use our intelligence as substrate for their own models — e.g. a bank builds credit scoring on in-market signal; FMCG builds demand sensing on category intelligence. We become an **intelligence supplier**, not only an answer engine.

## 3. Bring your own data

Upload or connect customer data; extract intelligence from **theirs × ours**. Their data read against the market backdrop only we can see — strongest differentiator vs generic AI tools.

## 4. Data & intelligence APIs

Partners who do not want briefs pull governed datasets, indices, and structured intelligence into their pipelines. Same gates, enforced at the API boundary.

## 5. Run on our infra

Deepest surface: clients run their models against our datasets **in place**. Model comes to data; raw signal never egresses; client receives intelligence output. Strongest governance posture and deepest lock-in.

## Invariant

Consent gates · k-floor · audit-and-metering ledger — identical across all five.
